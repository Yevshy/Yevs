    <h2>About Me</h2>

    <p>With 13 years of experience in design, I’ve been crafting web, mobile app, and product experiences long enough to remember when “flat design” was revolutionary. My journey started at 19, designing printable products like billboards, banners, business cards, menus etc.</p>

    <p>Fast forward to today, I’ve swapped paper cuts for pixels, creating user-centric interfaces and solving complex design challenges for industries ranging from e-commerce to real estate, medical services, CSR, marketing tools and others.</p>

    <p>While I enjoy exploring various design challenges, I thrive in long-term collaborations with substantial products where I can truly dive into usability and functionality.</p>

    <p>Figma is my go-to tool, though I still occasionally use Adobe Suite for tricky graphics. I’m also keen in animation, creating HTML5 banners and mobile app launchers using After Effects. When it comes to animation, perfect timing is my obsession.</p>

    <p>My approach combines classical design principles with a deep understanding of user behavior. I prioritise usability over flashy visuals, aiming to guide and support users through seamless interactions. That said, I am versatile and capable of delivering modern and eye-catching designs when required.</p>

    <p>Beyond design, I’ve managed cross-functional teams of up to nine members, taking responsibility for project outcomes and ensuring cohesive execution.</p>

    <p>Fast-learning and growth-oriented, I am always exploring new tools and methods to enhance my craft.</p>
